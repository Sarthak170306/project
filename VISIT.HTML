How to Get Here

Address: Bara Imambara, Chowk, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh 226003, India.

By Public Transport:

  • Metro: Chowk Metro Station (Red Line), ~5-minute walk
  • Bus: Bara Imambara Bus Stop, located right outside

By Car: Limited paid parking available on-site. We recommend public transport or ride-sharing during peak hours.

Accessibility: Wheelchair ramps, accessible restrooms, elevators, and parking available.
