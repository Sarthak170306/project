Events & Exhibitions

Join us for a variety of cultural, educational, and family-friendly events throughout the year.

Regular Events And Exhibitions

  • Cultural Festivals
    • Uttar Pradesh Day (26th January): Events focusing on Uttar Pradesh’s heritage and history.
    • Diwali or Eid Celebrations: Traditional lights, performances, and rituals.
  • Art and Photography Exhibitions:
    • Exhibitions about Lucknow's Nawabi era, artisans, and evolution.
    • Historical photography displays.
  • Music and Dance Events:
    • Classical music, Qawwali, and folk dance performances during festivals.
  • Workshops:
    • Awadhi cuisine, Chikan embroidery, and craft-based workshops.
  • Theatrical Performances:
    • Dramatic re-enactments of historical events and local legends.

Past Events Highlights

  • Lucknow Mahotsav (Cultural Festival):
    • Held in December–January showcasing music, dance, theater & cuisine.
    • Bara Imambara plays a key role in performances.
  • Classical Music and Dance Performances:
    • Held during national holidays like 15th August or 26th January.
    • Includes Kathak, Hindustani music, and folk presentations.
